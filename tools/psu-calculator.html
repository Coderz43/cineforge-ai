⚡ Component Power Analysis

  • CPU: 65–350W depending on model/usage
  • GPU: 75–450W for gaming cards
  • Motherboard: 20–60W
  • Storage: 5–10W per drive
  • RAM: 5–15W per stick

⚠️ Safety Margins

We add a headroom of ~30% to ensure stability during peak loads.

Recommended PSU: Calculated Load × 1.3

◎ Efficiency Ratings

  • 80+ Bronze: Good for budget builds
  • 80+ Silver: Better efficiency
  • 80+ Gold: Excellent (sweet spot)
  • 80+ Platinum/Titanium: Top-tier efficiency
