CineForge Toolset: Quick Overview

Explore purpose-built utilities for gamers, film fans, and PC builders. Each tool is lightweight, fast, and tuned for accuracy.

Game Finder — discover titles by mood & genre

Find PC and console games that match your vibe. Filter by genre, platform, and play-style to build a backlog that you’ll actually play.

Movie Finder — mood-based film search

Describe the feel (e.g., “uplifting sci-fi with heart”) and jump straight to relevant titles. Perfect for quick movie nights and niche deep dives.

Code Generator — instant snippets

Spin up boilerplate, utility functions, or API stubs in seconds. Great for prototypes, demos, and learning by doing.

PC Build Calculator — parts & budgets

Estimate total build costs, balance CPU/GPU, and avoid bottlenecks. Tailored suggestions for gaming, streaming, and editing.

PSU Calculator — safe wattage estimates

Right-size your power supply with headroom for future upgrades. Prevent instability, coil whine, and unnecessary over-spend.

FPS Calculator — performance by setup

Predict FPS for popular titles based on your CPU, GPU, and resolution. Plan settings before you buy or tweak.
